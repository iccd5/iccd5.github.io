NIST 800 Notes

Very few organizations are on the bleeding edge of technology. That's a good thing. By having a relatively standard technology infrastructure, you can rely on the work that has been done before you in the forms of best practices and, in this case, frameworks. Whenever possible, use these well established frameworks as guides in setting up your security program, these are tried and true measures that will help you avoid common pitfalls.

NIST 800 New Terms or Vocabulary

What is NIST

Key Terms

  • NIST-800: NIST’s 800 series presents information of interest to the computer security community. The series comprises guidelines, recommendations, and technical specifications.
  • NIST CSF: The Framework is voluntary guidance, based on existing standards, guidelines, and practices for organizations to better manage and reduce cybersecurity risk.
